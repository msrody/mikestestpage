Time to raise support.

April 8, 2019 By mike rody

It's been almost 6 years since we came to start our ministry here at IES.  We've been through 4 schools, 3 houses, 2 cars and 1 dog (that barely survived it's first month with us). Our kids have grown and grown.  We've endured hardships together, had some great trips and won a few victories along the way. Life is not always easy but we really believe God called us here and being where God puts you is the best place you can be.
